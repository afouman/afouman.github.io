Everyday Lazy Rules:

  1. Motion Priorities
    • Walking takes priority over running
    • Standing takes priority over walking
    • Sitting takes priority over standing
    • Lying takes priority over sitting
  2. Altitude Priorities
    • Going downhill takes priority over flat surface
    • Flat surface takes priority over going uphill
  3. If the object of interest is more than an arm-length away from you, you don't need it
